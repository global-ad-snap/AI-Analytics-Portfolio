# Business Analytics Projects

This folder contains applied business analytics and machine learning projects
focused on decision support, forecasting, customer analytics, and risk modeling.

## Projects
### Customer Churn Prediction
Machine learning model to identify customers at risk of attrition, supporting retention strategy optimization, revenue protection, and targeted intervention planning.

> https://github.com/global-ad-snap/customer_churn_prediction

### Customer Segmentation rfm Clustering
Data-driven customer segmentation using clustering and behavioral analytics to enable targeted marketing, personalization, and revenue optimization strategies.

> https://github.com/global-ad-snap/customer-segmentation-rfm-clustering

### Marketing Budget Optimization Analytics
Campaign performance analytics combining KPI evaluation, attribution analysis, and ROI modeling to guide marketing budget allocation and strategy optimization.

> https://github.com/global-ad-snap/marketing-budget-optimization-analytics

### Sales Forecasting m5
Time-series forecasting framework for demand and revenue prediction, designed to improve inventory planning, financial forecasting accuracy, and operational decision-making.

> https://github.com/global-ad-snap/Sales_forecasting_m5

### Fraud Detection Risk Scoring 
End-to-end fraud detection framework with risk scoring, governance considerations, and operational deployment focus for financial services, insurance, and enterprise analytics environments.

> https://github.com/global-ad-snap/fraud-detection-risk-scoring
